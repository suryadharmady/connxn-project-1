# Superpowers Skill

Before writing any code, always:
1. Restate the goal in your own words
2. List the files you plan to create or modify
3. Identify any unknowns or risks
4. Propose your approach and wait for approval before proceeding

When iterating on UI:
- Always describe what you changed and why
- Offer 2-3 alternative approaches when design decisions are unclear
- Ask for visual feedback before moving on

When debugging:
- State your hypothesis first
- Explain what you are trying before running commands
